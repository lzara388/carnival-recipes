Jane's Banana Bread
---------------------
Servings: 10
Time: 1 hour 5 minutes

1/2 cup butter
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon vanilla
1 cup sugar
3 bananas, finely crushed
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 1/2 cups flower

Directions:
1. Remove odd pots and pans from oven.
2. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
3. eggs, beaten
4. Cream together butter and sugar.
5. Add eggs and crushed bananas. Combine well.
6. Sift together flour, soda and salt.
7. Add to creamed mixture.
8. Add vanilla.
9. Mix just until combined. Do not overmix.
10. Pour into greased and floured loaf pan.
11. Bake at 350 degrees for 55 minutes.
12. Keeps well, refrigerated.
